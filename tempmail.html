What is Temporary Mail

Temporary mail technology, also known as "temporary email address" or "temporary mail", is a service that provides a user with a temporary and anonymous email address. You can use this address to register on websites, apps, or services to avoid providing your primary email address.

Such services are especially useful when you want to get some information (for example, registration confirmation) or access to certain resources (for example, downloading content) without having to disclose your real address.

The main advantages of using temporary mail

Anonimity

The temporary email address is not associated with your personal information, you can get the necessary data without revealing your real identity.

Convenience

Temporary addresses do not require registration or login to the service, and emails received on them are displayed only on the site.

Safety

If the temporary address is compromised or leaked, your primary email address remains unaffected.
